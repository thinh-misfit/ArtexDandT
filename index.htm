We heard you loved hand-made and natural?

Established in 1995, Artex D and T (so called Artex Duy Thanh) is proud to be one of the biggest companies in Vietnam in manufacturing and exporting handmade home wares and handicrafts.ections with unique designs in many several materials such as Bamboo, Rattan, Palm Leaf, Sea grass, Jute, Fern, Glass, Ceramic, and Lacquer.

Nature is cool!

Our offices locate in Hochiminh city, Hanoi capital and Danang city. You can visit us at our showroom in Hochiminh city as well as more than 10 factories in the North, Central and South of Vietnam. Our customers are from about 25 countries with more than 100 importers. Our products are now accepted widely at biggest markets like Germany, Finland, Spain, Japan, Korea, China, Singapore, USA and Brazil.
